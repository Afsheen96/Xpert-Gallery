Oriental food

The term "Oriental food" describes foods prepared and eaten in Southeast Asian nations. 'Oriental food' includes dishes from the Korean, Chinese, and Japanese cuisines. Their food uses a lot of dried fruits like apricot and plum as well as spices. Celebrity chef Akshay Nayyar asserts that stir-frying, boiling, and fast frying are some of the primary cooking methods used in oriental cuisine. These dishes frequently contain fermented sauces and pickled goods.
