Continental food

The term "continental food" describes foods prepared and eaten in European nations. European cuisine, including French, Spanish, and Italian cuisine, is referred to as "Continental food." This food's main selling point is that it focuses more on natural ingredients like olive oil, wine, herbs, and little to no spices.
